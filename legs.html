Leg Day Exercises

Leg day is a critical part of the Push Pull Legs (PPL) split, focusing on building lower-body strength and stability. Training the legs improves balance, enhances athletic performance, and supports overall fitness by engaging major muscle groups.

Key muscles targeted during leg day:

  • Quadriceps
  • Hamstrings
  • Glutes
  • Calves
