Push Day Exercises

Push day is a fundamental component of the Push Pull Legs (PPL) split, focusing on muscles that perform pushing movements. This includes chest, shoulders, and triceps. A well-structured push day helps develop upper body strength, improves posture, and contributes to balanced muscle development. The pushing muscles work together in many daily activities, making their training essential for functional fitness.

Key muscles targeted during push day:

  • Chest (Pectoralis Major and Minor)
  • Shoulders (Anterior, Lateral, and Posterior Deltoids)
  • Triceps (All three heads)
